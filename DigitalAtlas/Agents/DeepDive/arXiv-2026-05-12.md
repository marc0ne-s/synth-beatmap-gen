# arXiv DeepDive: 2026-05-12

## Silicon Showdown: Performance, Efficiency, and Ecosystem Barriers...
**URL:** https://arxiv.org/html/2605.00519v2
**Summary:** A comparative study of production-grade local LLM inference on Apple Silicon, evaluating various frameworks including MLX, MLC-LLM, Ollama, llama.cpp, and PyTorch MPS.

## Custom SIMD Kernels for Ternary Neural Networks
**URL:** https://arxiv.org/html/2605.06485v1
**Summary:** Presents custom SIMD kernels designed to eliminate multiplication for ternary inference. The implementation targets major CPU architectures, including Apple Silicon, to optimize throughput.

## An int4 KV Cache That Outruns fp16 on Apple Silicon
**URL:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.05699
**Summary:** Investigates the efficiency of quantized KV caches, demonstrating that an int4 KV cache can provide superior performance compared to fp16 on Apple Silicon devices.
