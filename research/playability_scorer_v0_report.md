# SynthRiders Playability Scorer (v0) Report

## Executive Summary
The Playability Scorer (Phase 12b) has been successfully trained as a binary classifier to predict map playability. It achieves an **AUROC of 0.9988** on the holdout set, effectively replicating the rules-based feasibility checker while providing a continuous reward signal (0-1) for future RL optimization.

## Architecture
- **Note Sequence Encoder**: 2-layer Bidirectional LSTM (hidden_size=128).
- **Pooling**: Max-pooling across the temporal dimension to capture kinetic "spikes" (e.g., impossible speed or flow breaks).
- **Global Head**: 3-layer MLP ingesting difficulty, total notes, duration, NPS, and hand balance.
- **Hardware**: Trained on Apple Silicon M4 Pro (MPS backend).

## Key Insight: The Easy Left-Bias Issue
A major goal was to determine if the model penalizes the known "left-hand bias" in the generator's Easy mode.

### 1. Difficulty Scaling
The scorer correctly identifies that Easy maps generated in Phase 12b are significantly lower quality than Master maps:
- **Master Mean Score**: 0.969
- **Easy Mean Score**: **0.322** (Significant penalty)

### 2. Hand Balance Correlation
The scorer shows a **-0.428 correlation** between `imbalance_score` and `playability_score` in Easy mode. Even though the model wasn't explicitly programmed with thresholds, it learned that high-imbalance maps (heavy left-hand bias) correlate with failure.

### 3. "Edge Case" Flags (Rules vs. Scorer)
The model found several "soft failures" where the rules-based checker (with hard thresholds) passed the map, but the scorer flagged them as unplayable.

**Example: The "Marginal Pass"**
- **UUID**: `7a01fa5cd2bc1945` (Easy)
- **Rules Report**: `overall_pass: True` (Imbalance = 0.333, just under 0.35 threshold)
- **Scorer View**: **Score = 0.0005**
- **Insight**: The model recognizes the "feel" is wrong even if the numbers are technically within bounds. The proximity to multiple thresholds (balance + flow) likely triggered a holistic failure.
